His Story

Sunjjoy Gupta is a renowned author, trainer and consultant who conducts workshops that blend deep academic knowledge with real-life industrial experience — offering a unique perspective that connects with every audience.

A results-driven professional dedicated to productivity and quality improvement, he holds a B.Sc. in Engineering (E&TC) and is a certified Six Sigma Green Belt. He is a Lead Auditor for OHSAS 18000 and a Qualified Internal Auditor for ISO & IATF, trained in the Core Tools of IATF, Train-the-Trainer and Achievement Motivation.

Through his years working with Japanese and Korean industries — in India and abroad — he has groomed students and company teams alike in continual improvement, ensuring enhanced customer satisfaction and long-term business success. A firm believer in giving back to society, he is a practitioner, writer and teacher all at once.
